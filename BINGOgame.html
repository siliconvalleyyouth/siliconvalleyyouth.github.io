Silicon Valley Youth BINGO! Game

Description:

Ahead of this semester, we're offering a hands-on tasting of our upcoming classes.

Whether you're interested in reading, coding, math, science, debate, or more, you'll find a spot in SVY Bingo!

Enjoy curated prompts from our teachers, prizes at every step, and a shot at our...GRAND PRIZE!!

There will be two levels of difficulty, Silver and Gold. Be sure to finish before our deadline, 11:59 p.m. 2/10!



Game rule:

  • Pick any level of puzzle (feel free to pick both!) and complete any line of 5, horizontal, vertical, or diagonal. Mark off each activity you complete to keep track.
  • Submit your results through the link below. Congrats -- you've won yourself a prize! Feel free to resubmit multiple times as you progress.
  • Each line from the silver level also earns you 5 points. From the gold level earns you 10.
  • Whoever has collected the most points by our deadline will also win a GRAND PRIZE!
