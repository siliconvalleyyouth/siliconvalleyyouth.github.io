What We Do

Classes

In order to broaden the intellectual and creative capabilities of middle to early high school students in the Silicon Valley, we teach classes of advanced topics that students would normally not see until the last years of high school, if at all. Our classes include Economics, Debate, Current events, US History, AMC 8, Java, Website Design, Public Speaking, and more.

See All Classes

Community Service

Our leaders and students are currently volunteering in East Palo Alto to help students there. We donate all profits from classes to Ravenswood City School District. In the past, we have raised over $50,000, with which we have donated a kiln, sponsored multiple writing contests, donated school supplies, and more.

Silicon Valley Youth is a student-run nonprofit educational and community service organization under the provisions of section 501(c)(3) of the Internal Revenue Code. Founded in 2015 by Bryan and Benjamin Owens, then students of Gunn High School. SVY was then led by Brandon Fu and Sam Yang and now is lead by Eric Yang and Oliver Ye.

Silicon Valley Youth is an official certifying organization for the President's Volunteer Service Award.

All profits are donated to the Ravenswood City School District and the Franklin McKinley School District.
