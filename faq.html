About SVY

What does SVY do?

SVY is dedicated to developing the intellectual, moral, civic, and creative capabilities of youth so they can serve and lead our society. SVY teaches classes to middle and elementary school students and donates the proceeds to underprivileged school districts.


Where do the proceeds from classes go?

Currently, SVY is working with underprivileged school districts including Ravenswood City school district and Franklin McKinley school district, and SVY is looking to expand its outreach to other organizations in the future.

Classes

I missed a class. How will I make up the material?

All of the class material and lecture notes are included in the recap email for each lesson. Teachers will also reach out to you to schedule a short make-up session before or after the next classes.

Why is my class being recorded?

At SVY we record classes to ensure quality. These recordings will not be accessible to the public, and will only be used for internal teacher training and review purposes by the SVY team only.


Can I get a link to a class recording?

No. SVY does not allow any class recordings to be played for students or any other public party. If you wish to make up material that you might have not attended, contact your teacher and they will provide you with the information you might have missed, or meet with you outside of class.

Refunds

How do refunds work?

SVY has a 1 class refund policy. After taking the first class, if you are unable to or do not want to attend the second lesson, we will provide you with a full refund. In order to qualify for a class refund, you must email us at svyfinance@gmail.com a minimum of 24 hours before the second class starts.


When can I expect my refund?

We will send a confirmation for your refund within 48 hours of your request. Due to the processing system that we use, most refunds take between 5-7 business days to complete.


Who do I contact to refund classes?

Please send an email to svyfinance@gmail.com and cc svyouth1@gmail.com. Include the following: a parent name, a student name, the name of the class, and a copy of the receipt you received.

Joining SVY Staff

Where can I apply to join the SVY team?

If you are interested in joining our team, we will be hosting teacher applications in between semesters. Teacher applications generally open in March and October, for the Fall and Spring semesters, respectively. If you would like to be added to our teaching staff mailing list, please email svyouth1@gmail.com and we will do so.


What are the requirements for becoming a teacher?

There are no strict requirements for becoming a teacher or teacher assistant, but it is very crucial that you demonstrate professionalism to teach a class, passion and deep understanding of the topic you are teaching, and responsibleness to uphold all teaching duties.


What are the steps in the application process?

The application process starts with a written application, and then the SVY Executive Team selects qualified applicants for an interview, from which teachers and TAs are chosen from.

Teacher Reimbursements

I am a teacher, and I want to give rewards to my students. How much money can I be reimbursed for?

The SVY policy for teacher reimbursements is $6 per student. This means if you have 7 students, you can be reimbursed for $42.


Where do I send my reimbursement request and what do I need to attach?

At the end of your class, please send an email to svyouth1@gmail.com and list your class name, who to paypal to, and receipts for your purchases.
